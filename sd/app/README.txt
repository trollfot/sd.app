INSTALLATION

 There's two ways to install SD in your plone instance :

  1. In 'portal_quickinstaller' (ZMI), simply select "Structured Document" and
     click install.

  2. In 'Site Setup' (Plone), click on 'Add-on Products' then on 'Structured 
     Document' and 'Install'


UNINSTALLATION

 There's two ways to uninstall SD from your plone instance :

  1. In 'portal_quickinstaller' (ZMI), simply select "Structured Document" and
     click uninstall.

  2. In 'Site Setup' (Plone), click on 'Add-on Products' then on 'Structured 
     Document' and 'Uninstall'
